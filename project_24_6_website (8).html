Skip to My Weekly Training

Running has always been a major part of my life. It’s not just a sport, it’s a discipline and a way to stay balanced, mentally and physically. From leading high school teams to following elite athletes, running is something I live and breathe.

I’m especially inspired by BYU’s collegiate running program. The Cougars have consistently been one of the top NCAA cross country and track programs in the nation. Watching runners like Conner Mantz dominate the 10K and qualify for the Olympics, and Kenneth Rooks win a national title in the steeplechase, motivates me to aim higher in my own training. Their hard work and humility reflect BYU’s values of discipline and integrity.
